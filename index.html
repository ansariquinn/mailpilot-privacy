Privacy Policy for MailPilot

Last updated: October 2025

MailPilot processes the text content of your Gmail messages solely to generate AI-based reply suggestions. The extension does not collect, log, or permanently store any personal data, email content, or message metadata.

MailPilot communicates securely with its backend API (smart-reply-zaua.onrender.com) only for the purpose of generating a reply or summary. All communication is encrypted using HTTPS and is used only to fulfill the user's request.

MailPilot does not sell, share, or transfer user data to any third party. We do not use your information for analytics, marketing, or profiling. All processing happens transiently while generating a reply or summary.
